Turning the power on

  1. Slide and hold the HOLD・POWER ON/OFF switch in the direction of “POWER ON/OFF” for more than 1 second.

    The “Accessing...” animation is displayed, and the IC recorder is turned on.

Hint

  • When you are not going to use the IC recorder for a long time, we recommend to turn off the IC recorder.
  • When the IC recorder is in the stop mode, and you leave the IC recorder without attempting an operation for a certain length of time, the auto power off function will take effect. (The length of time before auto power off is set to “10min” when you purchase the IC recorder.)
