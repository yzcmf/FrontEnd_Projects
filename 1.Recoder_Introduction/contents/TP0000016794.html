Monitoring sound while recording

If you connect headphones to the (headphone) jack, you can monitor the recording.

  1. Connect headphones to the (headphone) jack.
  2. Press VOL -/+ to adjust the monitoring volume.
    Monitoring volume does not affect to the recording level.

Note

  • When the cord of headphones touches the IC recorder while you are monitoring the recording, the IC recorder may record a rustling noise. The rustling noise can be reduced by holding the cord away from the IC recorder.
